PyeonGyeong app

PyeonGyeong is a stone percussion instrument.

Touch to play and adjust the sound in a variety of ways.
For example, you can adjust the volume by moving your finger vertically or horizontally with your finger touching the screen.

You can use the tuning function to easily play a scale that is different from the actual percussion.
